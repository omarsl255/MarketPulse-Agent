# Signal 2 — Academic Sponsorship Trajectory

## Idea

Track long-horizon R&D direction through public university partnerships, sponsored labs, fellowships, and research program announcements. Sponsorship patterns can indicate future talent pipelines and innovation priorities years before product impact is visible.

## Goal

Help ABB detect early research positioning by competitors to:
- monitor strategic themes (robotics, industrial AI, power electronics, edge systems)
- anticipate capability build-up and hiring direction
- inform long-term partnership and investment choices

## Workflow

1. **Plan targets**: define a watchlist of universities, labs, grant portals, and strategic keywords.
2. **Collect**: fetch university and corporate announcements, plus public grant data where available.
3. **Detect changes**: identify new announcements or material updates, not cosmetic edits.
4. **Extract**: capture institutions, topic, funding signal, timeline, and geography.
5. **Verify**: prioritize primary-source evidence and reduce confidence for secondary summaries.
6. **Store and surface**: persist events and expose trends for analyst review.
