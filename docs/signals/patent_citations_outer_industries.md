# Signal 3 — Patent Citations from Outer Industries

## Idea

Find competitor patents that cite prior art from industries outside core industrial automation. Cross-sector citation patterns can reveal adjacent capability moves before they are reflected in product messaging.

## Goal

Enable ABB to detect early cross-industry technology direction by:
- spotting unusual citation links (for example cloud, telecom, semiconductors, automotive software)
- tracking emerging patent clusters and technical themes
- prioritizing deeper scouting where evidence is strongest

## Workflow

1. **Plan targets**: define assignee profiles and sector filters for each competitor.
2. **Collect**: query public patent data for new filings and citation metadata.
3. **Detect changes**: identify new cross-sector citation edges vs prior snapshots.
4. **Extract**: capture focal patent, cited patent, sector distance, and strategic interpretation.
5. **Verify**: validate assignee mapping and reduce confidence when metadata is ambiguous.
6. **Store and surface**: persist events with stable public patent URLs for review.
